About Us

In short “INDFLEX”, is Kerala’s first and only ISO 9001:2015, NSIC Certified MSME unit, manufacturing hydraulic & industrial hose assemblies. Established in 1996, over the years INDFLEX has built an everlasting relationship with its customers, extending them the highest quality product & services. The assemblies manufactured at INDUS are used in automotive, industrial, marine, earthmoving & domestic applications. INDFLEX is equipped with Crimping Machines: Gates – GC32XD & POWER CRIMP 3000 and Uniflex with crimping range ¼” to 2 ½” ID withstanding a pressure upto 10,000 psi. Lot of weightage is given to the quality aspect since INDFLEX supplies to all major and minor industries across Kerala. INDUS TRADE LINKS since its’ inception, is the sole distributor and assembler for Gates products in Kerala. Gates Corporation USA, founded in 1911, is the world’s leading manufacturer of power transmission belts and a premier global manufacturer of power products (industrial & hydraulic hoses/end fittings and power transmission belts). A subsidiary of the parent company, Gates India Ltd; from Lalru, offers a complete portfolio of premium product and service solutions for both replacement and first-fit applications across targeted end markets, which encompass process and speciality, construction, agriculture, energy, transportation and automotive.

TRELLEBORG HOSES

Headquartered now in France, the company has its roots reaching as far as back to 1880s. Trelleborg Fluid Handling Solutions is a leading global manufacturer, proposing high performance and optimal solutions for every situation, including low and medium pressure industrial hose, oil and marine hoses, rubber sheeting and matting and expansion joints. These solutions are based on advanced polymer technology and are produced in France, Spain, Sweden and Brazil. Trelleborg offers a large range of competitive products, solutions and services that meet your needs and requirements for all types of applications and industries.

FLEXCO MECHANICAL FASTENING- A GIFT TO THE CONVEYING INDUSTRY

Flexco founded in 1907, headquartered in Illinois, USA has subsidiary locations in Australia, Chile, China, Germany, India, Mexico, Singapore, South Africa, and the United Kingdom. Holds 185 patents, over 1800 distributor partners throughout the world, supporting customers in more than 150 countries.

Flexco has developed real solutions to any conveyor belt challenges coming under the conveying industry. By constantly driving technology and design, Flexco strive to become the leader in belt conveyor solutions that maximise uptime, productivity and safety. To support the needs of Flexco customers, they partner with a network of the best distributors in every market they serve around the world.

Indus is the authorised distributor and fastener for Flexco Conveyor Belt products.

In short, Connecting Conveyor Belts with Flexco Mechanical Fasteners/Lacing/Endless joints and related Flexco belt accessories, and to focus on customers maximising belt conveyor productivity is our business.
